6.6. Email Report

The Email Report option allows you to manually send an email that contains the results for the selected race. This manual generation of the email will follow the rules and use the Email Settings defined on the General Config screen. For example if you have your email settings defined to send an email automatically to Admins Only then even if you click this button to manually send race results it will still only go to Admins.
