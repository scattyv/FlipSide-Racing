6.8. Delete Racer Results

To delete an individual racers results from a race you must first select the race in the table on the upper left side of the Statistics Screen. After the race is selected you then can select the racer in the summary table on the upper right of the screen and click Delete Racer Results. Clicking this button will cause a pop up message asking if you are really sure you want to delete the racers results. Clicking yes will delete the results or cancel will do nothing.
