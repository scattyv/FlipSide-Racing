6.7. Delete Race

Deleting a race is quite simple. You need to first select the race you wish to delete in the table on the upper left side of the Statistics Screen and then click the Delete Race button. The application will prompt you asking whether or not you are sure you wish to delete the race, click yes to permanently delete the race or cancel not to.
