8.4. Kyosho IC

The Kyosho IC lap counter is based on the Melexis 90121 RFID transceiver chip interfaced with an Atmel micro controller. The dNaNo and Kyosho tags utilize the 13.56MHz ISO15693 RFID standard for communication. The software uses a unique block of data stored on the RFID as the UID to identify each car.
